Space: The final frontier

Questions and Answers

Q:   Why did you make a website about Star Trek?    

A:     It was something I knew about. I used to not watch Star Trek but recently I've been getting into it. It's honestly not that bad a show though I understand why some people can't get past the first season or two of a given Star Trek show (there are multiple).

Q:   Why didn't you include the Romulans in meet the empires?    

A:     I thought about including the Romulans and even got a picture for them, but it felt like the list of empires was getting large and other than TNG and a little of DS9 the Romulans aren't mentioned much. I also thought about adding The Borg but decided not to because it's difficult to even call them an empire.

Q:   Why does this page have less Javascript than the other pages?    

A:     When making this page I already had a page that had wonky Javascript and techincal things. When it came time to make this one I decided to make a simple, nice looking page.

Q:   What was the most difficult part of making this website?    

A:     Understanding the flex grid. You'd think it would be easy and the concept is but i was surprised at how exact you needed to be, I constantly found myself having to tweak the little things, because so much as an element without an assigned grid space could throw off the entire page. I did eventually get it done though and i'm proud of the result.

Q:   Where did you get the background image?    

A:     The background image is a pretty interesting story. At one point I had looked up the smallest operating system and saw KolibriOs. One day I decided to download it and the background image was what you see now. The orignal image had a house island but to make it tileable I removed it. I at one point deleted the OS but decided to keep the background since I liked the image so much.
