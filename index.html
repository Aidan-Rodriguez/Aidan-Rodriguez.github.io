What Star Trek Is

Star trek is a series of tv shows and movies that take place in the Star Trek Universe. There are entres in the series and even I haven't been able to see them all. The Star Trek universe mostly takes place in a universe where warp travel is created. this allows for transportation across large areas and leads to a lot more species/cultures than we currently know on earth. Not all are friendly and not all are liked (in fact there's lots of wars going on in Star Trek) but interstellar trade is very common. Earth has unified as a planet that has elimated need with a seemingly infinite amount of food/energy. The Humans are also a founding member of the Federation of planets, which seeks to unify speacies and allow for peace among worlds.
